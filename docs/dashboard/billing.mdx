Add payment methods and manage your Context7 subscription on the [billing page](https://context7.com/dashboard/billing), which you can access from the dropdown menu in the top left corner of the dashboard.

![Billing page showing payment methods card and management options](/images/dashboard/billing-page.png)

![Billing page dropdown menu](/images/dashboard/user-dropdown.png)

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

1. Click "Add payment method"
2. Enter card details in Stripe form
3. Complete 3D Secure if required
4. Card appears in your list

![Add payment method modal](/images/dashboard/add-payment-method.png)

<Note>
  Your full card details are never stored by Context7. All payment data is handled securely by
  Stripe.
</Note>

### Managing Cards

**Set Default**: You can set a default card to be charged for future payments.

**Remove**: You can remove a card by clicking the delete icon.

![Manage cards modal](/images/dashboard/manage-cards.png)
